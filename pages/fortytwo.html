42 Rules

Objective

42 is a trick-taking domino game for 4 players in teams of 2. The goal is to be the first team to reach 7 points.

Setup

Each player receives 7 dominoes. The game uses a standard double-six set (28 dominoes).

Bidding

Players bid in turn, starting with 30 points. Each bid must be higher than the previous. The winning bidder chooses the trump suit (0-6).

Gameplay

The winning bidder leads the first trick. Players must follow suit if possible. Trump dominoes can always be played. The highest trump wins, or highest of the led suit if no trumps.

Scoring

Each trick is worth 1 point. Count dominoes (5-5, 4-6, 0-5, 1-4, 2-3) are worth additional points. The bidding team must make their bid to score a point.

Winning

First team to reach 7 points wins the game.
